About Us

Welcome to AS Repairs, where we are dedicated to providing top-notch desktop and laptop repair services. Our founder, Aryan Sheth, is a passionate tech enthusiast and a future Computer Science student. With a deep interest in technology from a young age, Aryan has always been fascinated by how things work and how to fix them.

Before starting this company, Aryan gained valuable experience working at Geek Squad, where he honed his skills in diagnosing and repairing a wide range of computer issues. This experience provided a solid foundation in customer service, troubleshooting, and technical repair skills. Fun fact, he programmed this entire website by himself!

At AS Repairs, we believe in providing personalized and reliable service. Whether it's a hardware issue, software glitch, or data recovery need, our goal is to get your devices back to optimal performance as quickly as possible. We belive in the entire repair being completed or your money back!

Thank you for choosing AS Repairs. We look forward to serving you and ensuring your technology needs are met with professionalism and expertise.
